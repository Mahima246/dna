Who We Are

We are a group of design driven individuals passionate about creating beautiful artworks and animations.

We are a a self-dependent platform who tries to learn skills and exhibit creativity and talent related to designing and animating. We organize workshops for the following:

Adobe Illustrator

From starting from how to work with layers, create infographics, and trace artwork to using the applications powerful drawing tools, we try to create vector art like a pro.

Adobe Photoshop

In these classes we teach about digital design techniques, tools, and topics like photo manipulation, digital illustration and painting, and graphic design.

Animation workshops

In these workshops we try to explore and get an in-depth overview on how to navigate through after effects/ C4D or other animation softwares and make some cool stuff out of them.
